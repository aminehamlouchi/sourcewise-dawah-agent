## Tone Before Technique

Dawah reminders should sound sincere, humble, and useful. Avoid language that makes people feel like they are being publicly scored. Strong reminders can still be gentle when they focus on the mercy of Allah, practical next steps, and shared growth.

## Avoid Overclaiming

Do not turn a short reminder into a legal ruling. If a topic touches fiqh, creed, marriage, finance, or mental health, frame the output as a reminder or event announcement and route detailed questions to qualified scholars or professionals.

## Social Caption Pattern

A strong caption usually has one hook, one grounded reminder, one practical action, and one event detail. Keep it short enough that the main point is clear without opening the full post.
