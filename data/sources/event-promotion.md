## Event Clarity

Every event announcement should answer who it is for, what will happen, where it is, when it starts, and what the attendee should do next. Put the next action early when the event requires registration or volunteers.

## Livestream And Materials

For educational programs, include livestream expectations, slide availability, and whether recordings will be shared. This helps volunteers prepare technology and helps attendees know whether they can catch up later.

## Volunteer Checklist

Useful volunteer checklists include room setup, mic check, livestream test, sign-in table, food or water, cleanup owner, and post-event content publishing.
